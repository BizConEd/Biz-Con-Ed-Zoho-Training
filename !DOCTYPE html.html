Communication With Suleiry – DO vs. DON'T

DO

  • Get to the point quickly
  • Use bullet points
  • State the outcome before the details
  • Ask: "What's the priority?"
  • Provide 2–3 solution options
  • Confirm understanding and deadlines
  • Give regular status updates
  • Be proactive and take initiative

DON'T

  • Write long paragraphs
  • Overexplain or include unnecessary detail
  • Delay action while "thinking it through"
  • Come with problems only
  • Assume she knows you're working on something
  • Wait for her to follow up
  • Take direct communication as frustration
  • Overcomplicate simple tasks

Best Format for Updates

Subject: Task + Status Body:
  • Current status
  • Risks/issues
  • Recommendation
  • ETA

Best Format for Questions

  • "I need clarification on…"
  • "Which option do you prefer?"
  • "Priority: High/Medium/Low?"
